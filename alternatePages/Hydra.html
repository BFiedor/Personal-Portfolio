Hydra (ENGR 422 Project)

Hydra was a semester long project on creating a fully operational Autonomous Hydroponic System.

The responsibilities of my team was the housing structure of the plants, the system water ran through, and the reservoir.

My specific role in this project was to program sensors (In Arduino) that would monitor the reservoir Temperature, and PH Levels.

Skills Utilized/Gained:

  • Breadboard/Microcontroller Experience
  • Team Management/Leadership
  • System Development and Design
